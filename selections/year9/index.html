WEGC Year 9 Course Selection Helper

Intensive

Intensive courses are taught entirely by one Learning Area (LA). Three are compulsory (they are already selected for you below). Choosing one of these courses counts two points towards the LA.

Connected

Connected courses are delivered across Learning Areas (LA). Two LAs have worked together to design courses that bring important learning for each area together. Choosing one of these courses counts one point towards each LA.

Summary

  • You will take 10 courses.
  • 9MAT, 9ENG, and 9HPE are compulsory so they are preselected.
  • You need to have at least 2 and no more than 3 points next to any Learning Area (except Maths and English).
