WEGC Year 9 Course Selection Helper

Intensive

Intensive courses are taught entirely by one Learning Area. Two are compulsory (they are already selected for you below). Choosing one of these courses counts two points towards the LA.

Connected

Connected courses are delivered across Learning Areas. Two LAs have worked together to design courses that bring important learning for each area together. Choosing one of these courses counts one point towards each LA.
